                  <div class="highlight"><pre><div class='line' id='LC1'>## Adafruit Eagle Library</div><div class='line' id='LC2'><br/></div><div class='line' id='LC3'>Now in Eagle 6 format!</div><div class='line' id='LC4'><br/></div><div class='line' id='LC5'>### Don&#39;t click on the files! </div><div class='line' id='LC6'><br/></div><div class='line' id='LC7'>[Click here](https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-Eagle-Library/zipball/master) </div><div class='line' id='LC8'>to download this as a zip file.</div><div class='line' id='LC9'><br/></div><div class='line' id='LC10'>### Installation</div><div class='line' id='LC11'><br/></div><div class='line' id='LC12'>1. Open Eagle and select the `Control Panel` window.</div><div class='line' id='LC13'>2. Choose `Options` and from the drop down that appears, `Directories`.</div><div class='line' id='LC14'>3.
